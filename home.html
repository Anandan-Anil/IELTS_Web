About Us

Make Your Dream Come True With edufair

A one-of-its-kind admissions service portal that has carved a renowned name in the Edtech landscape, EDUFAIR believes that goal of educational counselling is accomplished only when students receive the best possible education regardless of borders and financial constraints. With our unique Student first approach, EDUFAIR counsellors, mentors and head coaches guide students in finding the best course and university as well as sorting the admission process and submitting an impressive application to their chosen university. We help students connect with a mentor in their field who familiarizes them with the suitable course, career prospects and admission process .
